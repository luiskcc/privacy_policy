KerchTech Privacy Policy

Last updated: 01/10/2024

1. Introduction

KerchTech ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our clients and users ("you"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our web development services, interact with our website, or provide information through our Typeform surveys.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages
  • Project-related Information: Website requirements, design preferences, content
  • Typeform Survey Data: Responses to questions in our Typeform surveys

2.1 Typeform Data Collection

We use Typeform, a third-party service, to collect information from our clients. When you complete a Typeform survey:

  • Your responses are collected and stored by Typeform
  • We receive and store the information you provide in the survey
  • Typeform may collect additional data such as IP address and timestamp

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and improving our web development services
  • Communicating with you about your project
  • Sending updates, newsletters, and marketing materials (with your consent)
  • Analyzing usage patterns to enhance our website and services
  • Customizing our services based on your Typeform survey responses

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers (including Typeform) who assist us in operating our business
  • Legal authorities when required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business partners with your consent

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. Please note that Typeform has its own security measures in place for the data it collects and stores.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (where applicable)
  • Request a copy of your Typeform survey responses

7. Third-Party Services

Our use of Typeform is governed by their Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review Typeform's privacy practices.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
